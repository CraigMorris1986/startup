FAQs

What shopping centres are we in?

We are currently based in Townsville Queensland Australia at these major shopping outlets;

  • Stocklands Aitkinvale
  • Willows Shopping Town
  • Castle Town

Who are the Teamsters?

Here at Personal Shopper we are an equal opportunity employer and our Teamsters are the people who will go into the stores to check and purchase items for you and bring them to your car. All of our Teamsters undergo a background check before employment with Personal Shopper to ensure that we only employ trustworthy people for your peace of mind.

What are our operating hours?

As shopping centres have separate operating hours so do our Teamsters for each shopping centre. Our operating hours are from 9am to one hour after the last store in the mall closes. So if the shops close at 5:30pm our Teamsters are still there until 6:30pm to bring items to your car!

What makes us different?

Personal Shopper is an independent company, this allows us to do your shopping in any store that is within the shopping centre of your choosing. It does not matter if you need items across multiple stores our Teamsters are happy to make that run around for you.

Our live chat system also allows us to communicate with you on the fly, allowing you to make decisions with our Teamsters as if you were right there.

What are our plans for the future?

Our ultimate goal is to provide the most convienant shopping experience for our customers so that they are not waiting their valuable time. To this end we plan to operate within every major shopping centre in Australia and eventually expand globally.

We also eventually have plans to allow us to LayBy items and make those payments on our customers behalf and even deploy Teamsters to outdoor shopping events such as fairs and outdoor markets.
