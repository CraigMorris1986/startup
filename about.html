What are we about?

Here at Personal Shopper we understand that your time is very important and not to be wasted. Have you ever made a trip to the mall only to discover that what you wanted wasn't there, or wanted to go see what's new only to be disappointed that there is no new stock?

I know we have.

Personal Shopper has dedicated Teamsters that work in the malls awaiting your requests. Whether it is just to quench your curiosity or to do your shopping for you Personal Shopper is here to make your shopping experience as good as it was for you to be there in person without the hassle of being there yourself? Just by using your mobile phone or computer you can communicate live with our Teamsters in the malls to check in stores for you, purchase the items you need, and load them into your car in the car park as you pull up. Its the easiest shopping experience you will ever have!

We can fight those holiday crowds in your place!

We can do your shopping for you while your still at work!

We can check if a store has the thing you have been looking for!

We can even even drop your items off to you in your car in the malls car parks.
